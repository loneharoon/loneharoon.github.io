I am a Ph.D. student in the Computer Science and Engineering department at Indraprastha Institute of Information Technology - Delhi, India. I work with Prof. Pushpendra Singh. I work on energy sustainability within smart buildings. Specifically, my work is to detect energy wastage instances and to provide novel solutions to such problems.

Prior to joining Ph.D., I did my masters from National Institute of Technology Rourkela, India. During masters, I worked with Dr. Ashok Kumar Turuk. Main focus of the thesis was to localize sensor nodes both in static as well as in mobile scenarios.
